Madrid Flower Bloom Radial

A small visual project exploring seasonal flower bloom patterns in Madrid. This repository contains multiple experiment versions so I can compare design, structure, and interaction choices over time.

exp_0

Original all-in-one prototype

The original version, where layout, styling, data, and JavaScript logic are all contained inside a single HTML file.

Open exp_0
exp_1

Data-separated version

A more flexible structure where the flower data lives in a separate JSON file and the graph logic is moved into JavaScript.

Open exp_1

Flower dataset overview

The bloom periods below are approximate and synthesized from multiple sources. Some entries are based on public articles, while lilac was added from personal observation in Madrid.

Flower Approx flowering dates Approx peak Where to see them Reference site
Almond Blossom Mid January – late February Early February Quinta de los Molinos, Casa de Campo, Parque del Retiro (Huerto Francés), Jardín Botánico Silvosa Hermanos
Poppy (spring) April – May Late April Morata de Tajuña, Vega del Jarama, Polán (Toledo) Madrid Secreto, Travel Vibes
Poppy (autumn) September – October Mid September Morata de Tajuña, Vega del Jarama, Polán (Toledo) Madrid Secreto, Travel Vibes
Cactus Summer Late July Desert City Madrid Secreto, Travel Vibes
Cherry Blossom March – April Late March – early April Parque Juan Carlos I Silvosa Hermanos
Plum Blossom (ornamental) March – April Early April Parque Juan Carlos I, streets of Madrid Silvosa Hermanos
Lilac April April Parque del Retiro, parks and gardens in Madrid Personal observation
Sunflower Mid August – early autumn Late August Brihuega, Altos Campos de Gómara (Soria), Campos de Belmonte (Cuenca), Castillo de Malpica del Tajo (Toledo) Madrid Secreto, Travel Vibes
Lavender June – July June and July Brihuega, Pezuela de las Torres Madrid Secreto, Travel Vibes
Daisy March – September Late May Parque del Retiro, El Capricho, Parque del Oeste, Madrid Río Madrid Secreto, Travel Vibes
Rose Spring (sometimes also autumn or year-round depending on variety) Early June Rosaleda del Parque del Oeste, Parque del Retiro Madrid Secreto, Travel Vibes
Tulip Late March – early June Early April Real Jardín Botánico Madrid Secreto, Travel Vibes
